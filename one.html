Our Courses

BALLET:

Young dancers will be encouraged through a beautiful ballet curriculum. It is taught with a modern approach resulting in classes that are fun and enjoyable while remaining disciplined. Technique and training are essential, the student will focus on muscle awareness, posture, and placement.

Enroll Now

JAZZ:

Students learn to move with style and expression through jazz dance. Students enjoy the fast-paced nature of the class, while they learn intricate step patterns, jumps, and turns. It focuses on posture, technique, and flexibility. Classes are age-suitable and encourage enthusiasm and muscle awareness.

Enroll Now

HIP HOP:

It is a form of dance that combines complex body movements, choreography for music videos, and street style. As a result, it is a very popular style of dance in the world today. Class begins with a tempo-raising warm-up, followed by an isolated movement section and a set of contemporary dance moves. Students are inspired to incorporate their own style with the choreography, which allows for originality.

Enroll Now

TAP:

Footwork is all it is! This lively, rhythmic dance form recasts the performer into not only a dancer but also a percussive musician. Tap involves intricate footwork performed in the classical tap style.

Enroll Now

CONTEMPRARY:

Contemporary dance places a serious focus on the relationship between mind and body, with dancers being prompted to explore their feelings through dance. In contemporary dance, students are boosted to find their natural energy in order to produce dances which are often very personal. This style of movement often involves a great deal of playing with balance, floor work, fall and recovery, and extemporisation.

Enroll Now

MUSICAL THEATRE:

Musical Theatre is the actual threefold deadly program combining the art of singing, acting and dancing together. Acting exercises are used to promote improv and trust ensuring that all of the dancers develop greater self-confidence when performing. Students will receive training in all three disciplines, but mainly focusing on the dance.

Enroll Now
